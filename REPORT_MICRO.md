## 9. Methodology

Composite micro-score (0-100) per name = momentum 18% (price vs 50/200DMA + 52w-range position) + commodity-alignment 18% (material bias × OM linkage 0-9) + deep-dive 14% (evidence-based judgment) + analyst 13% (consensus + PT upside) + **fundamentals 12%** (TrueNorth annual: EBITDA margin, ROIC/ROE, leverage, growth) + news sentiment 9% + SmartScore 8% + quality 8% (cap/role/mcap). Missing sub-scores redistribute weight; `coverage` shows how much of the model was available. **High score = long candidate, low score on a liquid name = short candidate**, ranked within material group. Rebuild via `scripts/build_universe.py` → `scripts/gen_deep_auto.py` → `scripts/micro_build.py`; rendered in the dashboard's **Stock Picks** tab.

Each ticker's card now carries five data layers: **market snapshot**, **live IBKR position**, **recommendation + justification**, **fundamentals** (TrueNorth FY metrics — profitability, balance sheet, growth for **104/204** names — every held, recommended, large-cap and US-listed name TrueNorth covers), **underlying-asset exposure** (OM linkage 0-9 broken into revenue-exposure / price-sensitivity / coupling, each 0-3, with evidence), plus the commodity backdrop, thesis, catalysts, risks and sourced evidence. **All 204 scoreable names carry an evidence-based deep-dive from Bigdata.com** (news / filings / transcripts, cited with source + date) — 0 model-derived placeholders remain. Untradable listings (Norilsk/Moscow, Lima-only, sanctioned) are score-capped and flagged. Conflicting evidence is surfaced, not hidden — e.g. Berenberg's Buy upgrade on Anglo American Platinum is flagged against the PGM-short bias; Centerra's +160% YoY earnings noted against its short-lean; Buenaventura's strong margins/ROE noted alongside its revenue-miss/financing-overhang thesis.

The 6 names without a fundamentals block are genuinely unfetchable, not skipped: NVL and HNDNF return "not found" in TrueNorth (ADR-only tickers it doesn't track), NGC is a shell company with stale 2021 filings, EMM (Giyani Metals) and MINSURI1(Lima) are foreign-exchange-only listings TrueNorth doesn't cover, and KARS is an ETF (fundamentals don't apply).

*Caveats: analyst PTs across the metals complex are partly stale after the June-July correction (upside percentages inflated); foreign-listed names have no TipRanks coverage; 12 tickers carry a `quoteSuspect` flag (renamed/delisted mappings like GOLD→B, ACH, NVL); Bigdata.com content is news/filings-derived, not investment advice.*

### Yahoo Finance cross-validation

Each card shows an independent **Yahoo Finance cross-validation** (`yfinance`): it compares the primary FMP/TipRanks/TrueNorth numbers — analyst price target, rating direction, EBITDA margin, price — against Yahoo's, and flags divergences (agree / mixed / **conflict**). This is a data-integrity check, surfacing when two independent sources disagree on a name. (Google Finance has no free API, so Yahoo is the second source.) It is **display-only** — it does not feed the composite score. Rebuild adds `scripts/micro_yahoo.py` → `scripts/micro_build.py`.
